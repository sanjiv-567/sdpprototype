How much does Smoobu Dynamic Pricing cost?

We invite you to experience the full potential of Smoobu Dynamic Pricing. After the trial period, you'll be automatically charged unless you disable Smoobu Dynamic Pricing beforehand.

Pricing costs €12,99 per month per property. The amount is prorated for yearly and 2 year subscriptions. Yearly and 2-year discounts do not apply to Smoobu Dynamic Pricing.
